Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

SocietyOne ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the SocietyOne mobile application.

Your privacy is important to us.
We collect only the information necessary to provide secure and reliable society management services. We never sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address (optional)
  • Profile Photo (optional)
  • Society and Flat Details
  • User Role (Resident, Admin, Treasurer, Committee Member)

Payment Information

  • Maintenance Amount
  • Transaction Reference
  • Payment Date & Time
  • Payment Receipts
  • UPI Payment Details

We do NOT store your debit card, credit card or bank account information.

Documents & Media

  • Payment Screenshots
  • Profile Images
  • Society Documents
  • Uploaded Files

Device Information

  • Android Version
  • Device Model
  • App Version
  • Crash Logs
  • Diagnostic Data
  • IP Address

2. Why We Collect Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Verify society membership
  • Authenticate users securely
  • Generate maintenance receipts
  • Send notifications
  • Improve application performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

3. Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
Internet Authentication, data synchronization and cloud services.
Camera Capture payment screenshots and profile photos.
Photos & Media Select and upload documents or images.
Notifications Maintenance reminders, notices and alerts.

4. Third Party Services

SocietyOne uses trusted services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Google Play Services

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted communication, secure cloud infrastructure and access control to protect your information.

6. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information.

Your information may be shared only:

  • With authorized society administrators.
  • With trusted service providers.
  • When required by law.
  • To prevent fraud or misuse.

7. Data Retention

Your information is retained only as long as necessary to provide services, maintain records or comply with legal requirements.

8. Your Rights

You may request to:
  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account
  • Request data removal
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

9. Children's Privacy

SocietyOne is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion by contacting our support team. Data will be deleted unless retention is required by applicable laws.

11. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
